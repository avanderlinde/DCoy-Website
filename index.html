About DCoy Volleyball

DCoy Volleyball is a network of volleyball players, ranging from intermediate to competitive skill levels, and our mission is to provide opportunities for players from the Chicagoland area with an emphasis on quality game play and quality player connections.

Since 2006, we’ve been enjoying volleyball-related activities ranging from indoor open gyms to beach outings to tournaments. It all started with a small group of beach players but grew organically through word of mouth. We’re now a network of more than 900 outdoor and indoor players that continues to grow at an amazing pace.

No matter how the network grows and evolves, the focus will always be on the players. With good people, good games will come naturally. DCoy Volleyball, though, is more than just volleyball. In addition to spirited competition, there are plenty of lively social activities. Player interaction and networking plays a major role in defining this group. Players often develop long-lasting friendships with the people they meet on the courts. Some have found work opportunities, roommates, partners, etc.

It’s obvious that the opportunities within this network are endless. And we encourage you to take advantage of everything DCoy Volleyball has to offer. You will not be disappointed.
If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us!

Events Calendar

DCoy Volleyball offers some of the best open gym volleyball in Chicago. From October through April, players can indoor court time nearly every Saturday at Windy City Fieldhouse. Each game day, there will be a three-hour court, 12:30 - 3:30 p.m., for upper competitive volleyball. Sign up the week before as one of the 18 spots with your preferred positions and skill level. We then curate three teams to have a balance of position players and to be as evenly matched as possible. Games are rally to 15, so it's fast-paced; enough time to rest your legs but fast enough that you stay warm. Because of our format and reputation for good volleyball, we tend to attract skilled volleyball players that are also friendly and fun to play with.

Check in during the summer for great beach and grass tournaments.
